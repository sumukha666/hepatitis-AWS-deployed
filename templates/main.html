Liver Disease

Any condition that prevents liver from normal functioning. Liver problems can be caused by variety of factors such as viruses, alcohol and obesity.

Early Symptoms

>Jaundice
>Abdominal pain
>Chronic fatigue
>Nausea
>Decreased appetite

Risk Factors

>Heavy alcohol use
>Obesity
>Unprotected sex
>Family history of liver disease
>Shared injection needles

Maintain Healthy Liver

>Drink alcohol in moderation
>Get vaccinated
>Protect your skin
>Maintain a healthy weight
>Use medications wisely
>Keep hydrated
